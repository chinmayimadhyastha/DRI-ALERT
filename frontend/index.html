How It Works

Our advanced AI-powered system uses multiple detection methods to ensure maximum accuracy and reliability.

1

Camera Detection

High-resolution cameras monitor your eyes and facial expressions continuously while driving.

2

AI Analysis

Machine learning algorithms analyze patterns in real-time to detect signs of drowsiness and fatigue.

3

Instant Alert

When drowsiness is detected, the system immediately alerts you through audio, visual, and tactile warnings.

4

Safety Action

The system can automatically adjust vehicle settings or suggest rest stops to ensure your safety.
